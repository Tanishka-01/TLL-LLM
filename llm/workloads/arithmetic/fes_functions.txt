AVAILABLE FUNCTIONS:

add(a, b)
  - Adds two numbers together
  - Arguments: a (number), b (number)
  - Returns: The sum of a and b
  - Example: add(5, 3) returns 8

subtract(a, b)
  - Subtracts b from a
  - Arguments: a (number), b (number)
  - Returns: The difference (a - b)
  - Example: subtract(10, 4) returns 6
